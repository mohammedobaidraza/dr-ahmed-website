Your Journey

A Clear Path Back to the Life You Love

From the day of surgery to getting back on the golf course — here’s what recovery looks like with Dr. Ahmed.

Day of Surgery
Up and Walking

Most patients stand and take their first steps within hours. Many go home the same day.

Weeks 1–2
Moving at Home

Walking, stairs and therapy focused on motion, confidence and comfort.
